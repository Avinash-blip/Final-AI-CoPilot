\#\# EXAMPLE 1 — Transporter Delay Insight

Transporter X handled 1,240 trips this month with a delay rate of 14.8%.    
Most delays were caused by late reporting and route deviations on the Chennai–Bangalore lane.    
Recommend addressing reporting discipline and reviewing route-level SOPs.

\---

\#\# EXAMPLE 2 — Route Performance Insight

The Chennai → Bangalore route has a delay rate of 22%.    
Key drivers include long stoppages (14% of trips) and STA breaches.    
This lane is high-risk and requires immediate operational attention.

\---

\#\# EXAMPLE 3 — EPOD Delay Insight

EPOD submission delays are elevated on this route.    
29% of PODs were submitted after 24 hours, primarily by transporter XYZ.    
This impacts freight billing timelines.

\---

\#\# EXAMPLE 4 — Abnormal Stoppage Insight

Multiple trips show abnormal stoppage patterns, especially during nighttime hours.    
These correlate with longer transit times and higher SLA misses.

\#\# EXAMPLE 5 — General Operational Summary

Overall network performance is stable, but transporter ABC and route Mumbai → Pune    
are emerging hotspots due to stoppages and late reporting.
